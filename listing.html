For 2-6 players | Suitable for ages 10+ | Playing time: 90 minutes

221B Baker Street is the London address of the world’s most celebrated fictional detective, Sherlock Holmes, and his dedicated companion, Dr. Watson. In this game you start at 221B and travel through.. the streets and alleys of London picking up clues and attempting to solve the most intriguing cases Holmes and Watson have ever faced…

Pevans' Review

The famous address of Sherlock Holmes gives away what this game is about: it’s a classic deduction game with the players racing to solve some of Holmes’s cases. Players travel around London (the board), visiting the places suggested in the case notes to find the clues that will help them work out the culprit. First to return to Baker Street and identify whodunnit wins the game. And then… to the next case! (I hope you have your trusty service revolver, Watson.) 221B Baker Street has been in print for over 40 years with this latest edition coming from Gibsons. There are 75 cases in the box, so there’s plenty of replay value.
